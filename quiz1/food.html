LOCAL FOOD!

Kediri has a lot of unique and delicious food. One of the most famous food is "Tahu Takwa", it is came in the 1900s by the immigrants from China. Where whenever they have a festival, they always make a lot of tofu.
Fun fact! the tofu at the time from china was colored white, but since in kediri their building are mostly colored yellow, they decided to color the tofu yellow as a symbol of friendship between the two countries.
